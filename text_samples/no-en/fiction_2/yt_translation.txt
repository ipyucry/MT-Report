Optimists

The phone book is getting thinner and thinner. People disappear from photography and never come back. Doors unopened for years are lost in the bushes and flowers on the wallpaper. Centuries pass in an hour by the kitchen window. Alone in his cabin, Columbus begins a letter: “India must be paradise—impossible to find until one is dead.” Nevertheless, Columbus found India. In the same way that we find the truth about our lives and get up every morning with renewed energy. A toolbar in front of the map of terra incognita. The Craftsman turns the hourglass: every grain of sand falls like a stone.
