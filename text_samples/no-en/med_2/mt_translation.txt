Demographic data from 270 blood donors who were approved for plasma donation are presented in Figure 1, and the disease scores for 265 of these in Table 1. 143 (53 %) were men with a median age of 47 years (35–53). The median age for women was 42 years (29–52). The median disease score was 5 (3–6) (Table 1). The combination of cough, fever and dyspnoea was reported by 89/265 (34 %), of whom 53 (60 %) were men. 5 donors reported confirmed pneumonia, and 12 donors had been hospitalised.
