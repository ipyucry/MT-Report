ยง 6.Prohibition of discrimination
Discrimination on grounds of sex, pregnancy, maternity or adoption leave, care, ethnicity, religion, belief, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, gender expression, age or any combination thereof is prohibited. Ethnicity means national origin, descent, skin color and language, among others.

The prohibition covers discrimination on grounds of existing, presumed, past or future circumstances as mentioned in the first paragraph.

The prohibition also applies if a person is discriminated against because of his / her association with another person, and the discrimination occurs because of circumstances as mentioned in the first paragraph.

Discrimination means direct or indirect differential treatment pursuant to sections 7 and 8 which is not lawful pursuant to sections 9, 10 or 11.
