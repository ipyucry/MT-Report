2.1.1.1 If after the Debt Write-off under the Credit Agreement in accordance with the requirements of the Program the Bank does not receive the Debt Write-off Subsidy by 20.02.2011 (inclusive), the Debt Write-off condition shall be deemed not to have occurred. In this case the Borrower undertakes to repay to the Lender the amount of the Principal Debt in the amount existing as of the date preceding the date of Debt Write-off (hereinafter referred to as the “Recovered Debt” or “Recovered Debt”) by 30.06.2015 (inclusive) and at the same time to pay interest on the amount of the Recovered Debt in the amount of the Standard Lending Rate from 01.04.2015 to the date of repayment of the Recovered Debt by the Borrower to the Lender. The Bank shall send a notification to the Borrower on non-receipt of the Subsidy on write-off and on Debt Restoration not later than 21.06.2015.
