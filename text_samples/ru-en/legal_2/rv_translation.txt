2.1.1.1.	 If after the Cancellation of debt under the Credit Agreement in accordance with the requirements of the Program, the Bank will not receive the Subsidy on write-off until 20.02.2011 (inclusive), the condition for the Cancellation of debt is considered non-settled. In this case, the Borrower undertakes to repay the Creditor the amount of the Principal Debt in the amount existing on the date preceding the Debt Cancellation Date (hereinafter - «Recovered Debt» or «Debt Recovery»), by 30.06.2015 (inclusive) and simultaneously pay interest on the amount of Recovered Debt in the amount of the Standard Lending Rate from 01.04.2015 to the date of repayment of the Recovered Debt by the Borrower to the Creditor. The Bank shall notify the Borrower of the non-receipt of the Grant on write-off and of the Debt Recovery no later than 21.06.2015.
