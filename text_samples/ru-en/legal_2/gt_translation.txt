2.1.1.1. If after the Debt Write-off under the Credit Agreement in accordance with the requirements of the Program the Bank does not receive the Write-off Subsidy by 20.02.2011 (inclusive), the condition on the Debt Write-off is recognized as not having occurred. In this case, the Borrower undertakes to return to the Lender the amount of the Principal Debt in the amount existing on the date preceding the date of Debt Write-off (hereinafter referred to as the "Recovered Debt" or "Debt Recovery"), by 30.06.2015 (inclusive) and simultaneously pay interest on the amount of the Recovered Debt in the amount of the Standard Lending Rate from 01.04.2015 until the date of return of the Recovered Debt by the Borrower to the Lender. The Bank shall send a notification to the Borrower regarding non-receipt of the Write-off Subsidy and Debt Recovery no later than 21.06.2015.
