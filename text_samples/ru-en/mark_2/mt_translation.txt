The project will consist of several formats: a game, a checklist, and content material.
Content material
A longread, in which we will tell you how a resident of a metropolis can prepare for winter.
It will be divided into two parts: advice and personal opinion.
In the part with tips, we will list life hacks related to car care (for example, a reminder to pour water out of the wiper tank in advance so that it does not freeze with the first frost, as well as put a brush and scraper in the car, etc.).
In addition, it will talk about the importance of choosing tires that will ensure safety on the road - this entire paragraph will be devoted to the advantages of XXXXX tires.
